# Lesson 7: Escaping the Precision Trap in Orchestration

## The Concept
Based on the **Language as Momentum** research, we discovered that simple "model pass-through" isn't enough to solve truly difficult problems. Single models tend to get stuck in "local minima" (logical dead ends) because they are trapped by the precision of their own training gradients.

## What Rhea Does
The **Rhea Pod** (Socratic Debate) engine was specifically designed to implement the "discontinuous jumps" discussed in the research. By forcing three models into specialized roles (Dreamer, Doubter, Decider), Rhea creates **structural serendipity**.

## The Lesson
Orchestration isn't just about reducing cost or increasing speed; it is about **mathematical momentum**. Converting internal model states into discrete tokens (language) and passing them to a second model with a different latent geometry is the only way to find solutions that exist outside a single model's predefined landscape. Rhea is the bridge that facilitates these "teleportations" across the logic landscape.
